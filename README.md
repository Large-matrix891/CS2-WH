### Aimbot Controls
The aim assist module helps you align your shots on targets. You can choose between smooth aim for a natural feel or snap-based movement for quick reaction. Hitbox priority allows you to specify whether you aim for the head, chest, or stomach area.

### Visual Tracking
See enemies through wall textures with the ESP overlay. This feature draws boxes around players, shows their remaining health, and identifies the weapons they carry. You can toggle the skeleton view to see the exact posture of other players during matches.

### Sound and Trigger Actions
The software monitors game audio signals. When the game registers a specific sound, such as a bomb plant or a footstep, the application notifies you. You can set the triggerbot to fire your weapon immediately when an enemy enters your crosshair.

### Advanced Settings
You can modify the environment lighting and skybox colors to improve visibility in dark corners. These settings adjust the brightness and contrast of game textures. You can also hide certain visual elements to make the game screen appear cleaner and more manageable.
